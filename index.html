Common Questions

Is this just another dumb chatbot?

Not at all. Old chatbots followed rigid decision trees and frustrated customers. Our digital employees are custom-trained to understand context, nuance, and actually solve problems the way a real team member would.

How long does training take?

Typically 7-10 days. We do all the heavy lifting of ingesting your documentation, setting up workflows, and thoroughly testing scenarios before they ever interact with a real customer.

What happens if it doesn't know the answer?

We design graceful handoffs. If a request is outside its training, complex, or highly sensitive, it politely informs the customer and seamlessly routes the conversation to the right person on your team.

Will this replace my current staff?

Think of them as a teammate, not a replacement. They handle the repetitive grunt work, data entry, and basic scheduling so your human staff can focus on high-value tasks, strategy, and building real relationships.
