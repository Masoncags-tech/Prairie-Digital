Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Prairie Digital LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website prairie-digital.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site or use our services.

Information We Collect

Information you provide: When you fill out a contact form, schedule a call, or email us, we collect your name, email address, phone number, business name, and any details you include in your message.

Information collected automatically: When you visit our site, we may collect standard web analytics data including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring website. We use this to understand how people find and use our site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request
  • To communicate with you about our services
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • To send you relevant information if you have opted in to receive it

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., form processing, analytics, email). These providers only access your data to perform services on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

Form Submissions

Contact form submissions on our site are processed through FormSubmit.co, a third-party form handling service. Your submission data is transmitted securely and forwarded to our team via email. We do not store form data on our servers.

Cookies and Analytics

Our site may use cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to view our site.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Our site uses HTTPS encryption for all connections.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at hello@prairie-digital.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
